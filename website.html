      Breakfast in Hong Kong

      A typical breakfast in Hong Kong involves an egg tart, an egg and ham sandwich or noddles, and a cup of hong kong style milk tea. Many people also choose traditional wonton noodles that are all manmade, and although it takes a longer amount of time to create, it is definitely worth the wait! Many people also choose to eat pineapple buns or "bo lo you" in my native tongue. Most of the elders opt for none of the above and instead go to restaurants to "yum cha", which literally means to drink tea, but also means to eat dim sum and other Chinese food at a restaurant.
